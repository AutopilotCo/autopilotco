Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Autopilot Co ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Autopilot Co Etsy shop and associated Pinterest account. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any information when you interact with our products or services.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect personal information directly. When you purchase a product through our Etsy shop, Etsy processes your order and payment. We only receive the information Etsy provides to sellers, which may include your first name and the product you purchased, for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

Our Pinterest account is used solely to publish pins that link to our Etsy shop listings. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data through our Pinterest presence.

2. How we use information

Any information received through Etsy is used solely to deliver your digital product and respond to any support requests. We do not use your information for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.

3. Digital products

Our products are digital downloads delivered instantly through Etsy's platform. We do not store copies of your purchased files on any external server. All file delivery is handled entirely by Etsy.

4. Third-party platforms

Our business operates across the following third-party platforms, each with their own privacy policies:

Etsy — our primary sales platform. Etsy's privacy policy governs all transactions: etsy.com/legal/privacy

Pinterest — used to share product pins. Pinterest's privacy policy applies to all interactions on their platform: policy.pinterest.com/privacy-policy

Claude (Anthropic) — used internally to generate product content. We do not share customer data with Anthropic.

5. Cookies

Our Etsy shop and Pinterest account may use cookies as governed by those platforms. We do not set any cookies independently.

6. Data retention

We do not maintain any independent database of customer information. Any data held by Etsy is subject to Etsy's own data retention policies.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data held about you. For data held by Etsy, please contact Etsy directly. For any other queries, contact us using the details below.

8. Children's privacy

Our products are intended for adults running small businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of our products after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through our Etsy shop: etsy.com/shop/AutopilotCo
